AMH Predicts Oocytes — Not Live Birth

Key Message

AMH strongly predicts ovarian response and blastocyst yield but does not independently predict live birth once embryo ploidy is confirmed. AMH is a quantitative, not qualitative, biomarker of reproductive potential.

Interpretation

AMH exerts strong effects on oocyte quantity and blastocyst yield. However, these pathways do not translate to live birth — confirming that AMH is a quantitative, not qualitative, marker of reproductive potential.
